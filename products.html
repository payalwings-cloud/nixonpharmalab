Nixon Pharmalab products

Nixon Pharmaceutical Industries Ltd. (Nixon Pharmalab) will be a partnership form of business organization. We bring in the resources together, make the decisions, and manage the business which are based on sharing the profits and risks. We will be a multinational pharmaceutical company dealing with the manufacturing and selling of Capsules, tablets, creams, lotion, and ointments. We will specialize in "central nervous system" prescription products in addition to our OTC allergy relief medicine initiative. We sell and distribute the products based on standard and approval either from EMA or FDA in the US or EU market respectively. Our products are not limited to:

  • Tablets
  • Capsules
  • Ointment
  • Cream
  • Lotion
